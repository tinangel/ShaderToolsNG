This menu allows you to access your materials database and integrate them into your scene.
Click the 'Open ...' button in ShaderTools interface :
A new window appears, select the 'Bookmarks' panel and 'Materials Database':
A list should appear at the middle of window :
For a best visual representation of materials you can use the switch for displaying thumbnails :
You should get this list :
You will just select a material to import it inside your scene :
Then double-click it or use the Import button at the top right corner of your screen :
The window is now closed and your material should appears into list of materials :
